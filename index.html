Welcome to Smash Bros. Source

This site is a Smash Bros. hub with all the new information on the next game, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. We cover all the confirmed characters, stages, and assist trophies in the latest game along with the latest news and leaks. We even cover the most likely fighters that could make it into Super Smash Bros. Ultimate by going over their popularity and other aspects.


Release date: December 7, 2018

Characters Confirmed

That's right, every character that as ever been in the Smash Bros. series is back. No one's favorite character will be cut this time around, including even young Link and Pichu. Now, let us talk about the most exciting thing about Smash Bros., the newcomers. Though only two characters have been confirmed so far, they are among the most requested ones. Inkling was the first one announced the same time the game was revealed, along with the long time requested character, Ridley.

Stages Confirmed

Since we have gotten all character to return this time around, many people were wondering about the stages. Although not all stages will be returning, most of them will, along with brand new stages. So far, they seem to be based on games released on the Nintendo Switch. Two of the new stages they showed off were the Great Plateau Tower from the Legend of Zelda and Moray Towers from Splatoon.

Assist Trophies Confirmed

Many new assist trophies have already been announced covering many different game series. The ones they announced so far include Krystal, Knuckles, the squid sisters, Samurai Goroh, Waluigi(sadly), and Bomberman. Though your favorite character might not make the roster, you can still hope they make it as an assist trophy.
